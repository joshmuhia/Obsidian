Sparkling Taekwon-do

Taekwon-do classes are available for everyone the age of 4+, its never to late to learn. We also go for competions and events in countries such as, Uganda, Rwanda,
and we plan to expand to the USA in the near future. So if your learning from scratch or are looking to level up sparkling taekwondo is the place for you.
