Sparkling Taekwon-do

Sparkling taekwondo is an ITF Taekwon-do club founded by John Mwangi (1st dan) in 2016,
and it was founded so children all over the country may have a chance to learn this martial art.
This Website can help you child prepare for gradings, learn new skills,
disipline, self defence, self reliance, and independence in the everyday world.
